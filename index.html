Introduction

I hold a Master's degree in Computer Science from Dalian Maritime University (DLMU). Throughout my academic journey, I have specialized in the field of EDA (Electronic Design Automation) Simulation Software Testing. My primary research focus has revolved around the application of cutting-edge testing methodologies, including fuzz testing, various testing techniques, and equivalent testing, within the context of EDA Software.

Under the guidance of my esteemed mentor, Professor Shikai Guo, I have embarked on a journey to explore and innovate in the realm of software testing. Additionally, my academic collaboration extends to working closely with Professor Xiaochen Li and Professor He Jiang from Dalian University of Technology (DLUT). Together, we have been at the forefront of advancing the state-of-the-art in EDA Simulation Software Testing, contributing to the enhancement of software quality and reliability in this critical domain.
